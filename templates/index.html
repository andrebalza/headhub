Why Hiya?

Because finding the right hairdresser is a tough job.

  • EVER HAD A NIGHTMARE HAIRCUT?

    ...and then paid 100€ for the privilege? Hiya is designed to make it easier for you to find and book the right stylist, so you don’t have to keep explaining over and over the cut you want.

  • FINDING “THE ONE”

    A regular hairdresser will be someone who’ll understand your hair and style, every time. But this is something many of us are missing out on. We think that if you want to look your best, it makes sense to know about the person with the scissors! Googling reviews by strangers just isn’t up to the job. Even if you choose the salon, you still don’t really know anything about the stylist. That’s where Hiya comes in.

  • SETTING THE DATE

    This is where you make your commitment. You may not know what you want, or who exactly you’re trusting to do it, but you do know when. Well, 1 out of 3 isn’t so bad...is it? Of course it is! We think it’s better to know everything you need to, so you can make the right choice.

  • ON THE DAY

    You’ve got this far - it’s make or break. You sit in the chair, and the next few minutes talking to your stylist can define the next few months for you. Will you walk out happy, or wearing your hat? The consultation is crucial - but it’s sometimes very hard to explain what you want, or you might not even know. Hiya gives you a place to find inspiration and store your own photos so you can find and explain what you want, fast!

  • HAPPILY EVER AFTER?

    If you love your cut, then it’s a match made in heaven! But if not, you have to start the process all over again. Wouldn’t it be great if there was a service to help you with that…?

What is Hiya

Hiya takes the hassle out of haircuts! We’re a matchmaking service to find your perfect hairdresser.

Create your Style & Hair Profile

You know your hair and style better than anyone! So, Hiya let’s you create your own personal style & hair profile to help the hairdresser achieve the results that YOU want.

Simply Search, Book & Pay!

Hiya brings you the information you need to one place. You can search easily with keywords such as location, price, specialisation and more. Better still, we let you book and pay through our service, so your experience is about your hair, not hassle!

Collect your Haircut History

We’re a community who care about hair. Like your cut? Then take a picture! You can keep these all in the same place with our service, so next time you need a cut you can show what you want. That’s not all though. With Hiya, you can get inspiration from other user’s photos, collect references, check out a hairdresser’s portfolio, and give ratings too!

Hiya for Hair Professionals

Professional profile

With Hiya you can create your personal professional profile, and give a potential customer a full overview of your skills and expertise. You can add everything - description, services, prices, social media accounts, portfolios, location, contacts and more!

Showcase your work

Your work is your best advert! Add the finest hairstyles you have crafted onto your profile. Let potential customers see what you can do, and be inspired by your flair.

Know your clients

Not only do you get a profile, but so do your customers. You will be able to see their hair history, preferences and interests to better meet their needs and give a more personalised service.

Expand your clientele

Hiya brings the customers to you because it's a community that's all about hair! A good online presence is a great way to promote yourself and expand your customer base.

Connect to your customer

Hiya also helps you to keep customers connected to your business, so they're up to date with all your latest info and promotions. They can follow you on the app, so it's easy to keep your loyal customers even if you move to different salons.

Grow your portfolio

When you do a great job, you get a happy customer who shares their new look. That can be added to your portfolio, so they do the advertising for you!

Let them find you

All the information you put on your profile will be visible and searchable by the users. Your business can now be visible for the great skills you offer, and for the great feedback you've received from your customers!

Easy to use

Hiya is an easy-to-use mobile app. It only takes minutes to be added to it's thriving community, and growing network of potential customers.

Do what you love

Unique styles need a unique hairstylist! Hiya makes it quick and easy to reach customers, so you can concentrate on the hair not the hassle.
