What is Hiya

Hiya takes the hassle out of haircuts! We’re a matchmaking service to find your perfect hairdresser.

Create your Style & Hair Profile

You know your hair and style better than anyone! So, Hiya let’s you create your own personal style & hair profile to help the hairdresser achieve the results that YOU want.

Simply Search, Book & Pay!

Hiya brings you the information you need to one place. You can search easily with keywords such as location, price, specialisation and more. Better still, we let you book and pay through our service, so your experience is about your hair, not hassle!

Collect your Haircut History

We’re a community who care about hair. Like your cut? Then take a picture! You can keep these all in the same place with our service, so next time you need a cut you can show what you want. That’s not all though. With Hiya, you can get inspiration from other user’s photos, collect references, check out a hairdresser’s portfolio, and give ratings too!
