Why Hiya

Because finding the right hairdresser is a hard job.

  • Have you found the right one?

    Have you ever paid 120â‚¬ but the haircut didn't go how you planned? Each time you end up with a different stylist and you are tired to explain over and over again how you like your hair done. Maybe you are new in town or your trusted hairdresser moved away. Many of us are missing out on the benefits of having a regular barber or a hairdresser.

  • THE SEARCH PROCESS

    The usual story goes like this: you need a haircut for a special occasion or just a fresh look. You start googling and reading random reviews done by strangers or asking recommendations from your friends and family. After a time-consuming search process, you choose the salon. The salon might have good reviews, but you have no idea who is going to cut your hair.

  • BOOKING

    Next you need to book the appointment. You might use phone, email or online booking system depending on the salon. You might feel anxious, because you still have no idea how you would like your hair done, but the online booking system forces you to make a decision before you have spoken with your hairdresser.

  • CONSULTATION

    Once you have managed this far, it's time to show up at the salon. The most important phase of the service experience is the moment just before the haircut, where you together with your hair stylist decide what kind of a haircut you will have. The consultation will define whether you walk out the hair salon happy or wearing your hat. Sometimes it is very hard to explain what you want. You might have pictures of your old haircuts that took ages to find or you don't even know what you want and you would like your hairstylist to make the decision for you.

  • BOOKING

    If you are satisfied with your haircut, you will most probably return. But if your haircut didn't go how you planned, you have to start everything all over again. Could there be an easier way?
